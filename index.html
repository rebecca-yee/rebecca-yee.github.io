ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Rebecca! I'm a sophomore studying psychology at Rice University, located in Houston, Texas. I intend on graduating in May of 2024 with a B.A. in Psychology and a minor in Environmental Studies. After programming various web development and hackathon projects, I realized I had more of a passion for the UI/UX design area of product building--for getting into the mind of the user and optimizing the product to be as convenient as possible. I am interested in developing my design skills and learning more about the UI/UX process. In my free time, I enjoy reading, playing with my cat, Harvey, and jamming out to Taylor Swift.
